Important Information

GLOBIS HR CONSULTANCY FZE LLC requires the provision of the following documents to facilitate approval from the Department of Overseas Employment and the pertinent government ministries of the respective home countries in which we are located.

Required Documents

Essential paperwork for overseas recruitment

Document 1

Demand Letter

A letter should be addressed to GLOBIS HR CONSULTANCY FZE LLC, specifying:

  • Number of workers required in each category
  • Monthly salaries for each position
  • Contract period
  • Duty hours
  • Air passage arrangements
  • All other benefits for workers
Document 2

Power of Attorney

An authorization letter to GLOBIS HR CONSULTANCY FZE LLC to:

  • Conduct manpower recruitment
  • Handle all necessary formalities in arranging interviews
  • Sign required documents and employment contracts
  • Comply with home country regulations
  • Arrange for passports and travel
  • Facilitate travel to country of employment
Document 3

Offer Letter

This is an official letter that carries all details of job employment offer, which includes:

  • Job description
  • Salary package
  • Accommodation details
  • Medical insurance coverage
  • Air ticket provisions
  • Vacation entitlements
  • Other benefits and allowances
Document 4

Agreement Letter

This document outlines the terms and conditions of service between the employer and GLOBIS HR CONSULTANCY FZE LLC.

It establishes the formal business relationship and defines the responsibilities, obligations, and expectations of both parties throughout the recruitment process.
